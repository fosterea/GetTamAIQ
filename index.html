Combine Marin Schools to get Tam. Press space to restart.


HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys or swipe with your finger to move the tiles. When two tiles of the same shcool overlap, they combine!

NOTE: The order of schools is not intended to be a ranking. It is based on when the school was founded/built, with Tam being the oldest.
